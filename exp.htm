Sam B
Colchester, United Kingdom
14653
Reviewed 1 week ago via mobile
Great location, lovely food

Visited on a busy Sunday afternoon, the restaurant is in a great location just off Covent Garden.
Carluccios is on 2 floors with steps up to the main entrance.
We were seated in the 1st floor dinning room up a beautiful staircase.
Tables were quite close together but the room was lovely and bright with large windows overlooking the main street below.
The main menu plus a set price menu was offered to us by a friendly waitress.
Delicious bottle of house red wine plus a couple of cocktails ordered plus olives & bread.
The seafood linguini was gorgeous, cooked perfect. Highly recommend the lobster lasagne.
Our only complaint would be the lack of attention from the waiting staff, several times we had to asked for extra drinks, missing cutlery, side plates, condiments etc which delayed the eating of our food which was very frustrating.
All in all at £25 per head we had a delicious lunch and would definitely return to Carluccios again.
